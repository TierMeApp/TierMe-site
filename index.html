What is TierMe?

TierMe is the world’s first rarity-based self-assessment app. Input your life stats — like income, fitness, and skills — and get ranked by percentile across key categories. See your Tier Score, share your stat card, and compete to improve your life like it’s a game.

How It Works

1. Input Your Stats

Choose your categories: Looks, Wealth, Fitness, Career, Social, Skills, and more.

2. See Your Tier

Get scored by percentile and rarity: Common → Mythic. Know where you stand in every category.

3. Compete + Improve

Share your Tier Card, climb the leaderboard, and unlock tips to raise your rank.

Level Up Your Life

Your Tier is just the beginning. After you get your personalized ranking, TierMe helps you improve where it counts.

  • ✔️ Growth Suggestions: Unlock tips and habits tailored to your weak spots — whether it’s boosting income, gaining muscle, or building confidence.
  • ✔️ Tier Goals: Track your progress and set goals to reach the next rarity tier.
  • ✔️ Challenges & Milestones: Join weekly stat-based challenges like “Top 10% Body Fat” or “Most Improved Social Tier.”
  • ✔️ Compete with Friends: See how you stack up with your circle — or go global on the public leaderboard.
